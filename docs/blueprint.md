# **App Name**: ArtEcho

## Core Features:

- Artisan Profile Creation: Allow artisans to create and manage their profiles, including name, craft, location, and story.
- Product Upload: Enable artisans to upload products with photos, descriptions, prices, and tags.
- AI Story Card Generation Tool: Use AI to automatically generate a Story Card based on the artisan's profile and product details, offering a translated description and a QR code for audio.
- Product Browsing: Allow buyers to browse products in a visually appealing grid/gallery.
- Story Card Display: Display the artisan's story and product details in an engaging Story Card format.
- Connect with Artisan: Enable buyers to connect with artisans.
- Role-Based Dashboards: Provide separate dashboards for artisans and buyers.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: White (#FFFFFF) for base/background.
- Accent color: Terracotta Orange (#E2725B) for buttons, highlights, logo icon.
- Text color: Charcoal Gray (#333333) for text and strong contrast.
- Saturated purple (#A340D3) to convey creativity, uniqueness, and a touch of luxury.
- Background color: Light purple (#EEE7F5), a desaturated tint of the primary, to provide a gentle, non-intrusive backdrop that keeps the focus on the content.
- Accent color: Pink (#D340A3), an analogous color with the purple to produce a design that is cohesive and visually appealing
- Body font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif that combines a modern look and a little warmth or personality, suitable for body text
- Headline font: 'Playfair', A modern sans-serif similar to Didot, geometric, high contrast thin-thick lines, with an elegant, fashionable, high-end feel; if longer text is anticipated, use this for headlines and 'PT Sans' for body
- Use culturally relevant icons representing various crafts and regions.
- Employ subtle animations for page transitions, product card hovers, and loading states to enhance user experience.
